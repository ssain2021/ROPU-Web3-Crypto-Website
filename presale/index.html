    HOW TO BUY

    Note:

    1. You can't buy more than 5000 USDT, or less than 40 USDT in a single Transaction.

    2. USDT is disabled in Metamask Wallet ON MOBILE devices.

    3. Safari Browser can NOT be used in Mac/ Laptop, as Wallet Extensions are not allowed in Safari.

    4. If you dont have Metamask, Trust or Coinbase Wallets, then you can send to our Presale address (given above) from your any BSC-Network supported DeFi Wallet. Please intimate us in our Telegram Support.

    A. BUY Using Laptop / PC of Windows / Mac / Linux (Brave/ Chrome/ Firefox/ Edge)

    1. Open any of the Browsers - Brave, Chrome, Firefox, Edge

    (Note: For Firefox and Edge Browsers, Only Metamask Wallet Extension is Supported)

    2. Go to our Presale page (https://robopuppy.world/presale) to buy.

    3. Click 'Connect Wallet' Button. Select the wallet you want to Connect with.

    4. After connection, make sure your Wallet is in BSC Network.

    5. Select one of the Currencies- BNB or USDT. If you select USDT, You still have to have little BNB for Gas Fees.

    6. In the 'Pay BNB' or, 'Pay USDT' field, give the amount of the (USDT/BNB) you want to spend. In the next field ('Get ROPU') You will see the amount of ROPU that you would get.

    7. Then click 'Buy ROPU' button. A loading screen will appear. Wait until 'Buy' popup from your Wallet will appear. Scroll down in the popup.

    8. If you encounter an Error now, or before, then follow the instructions stated in the error. If you still face issue, please contact us in Telegram Support.

    9. If you see 'Succesfully bought ROPU' alert, it's DONE! Congrats on buying ROPU. Then your page will refresh, and you can see your ROPU Balance below BNB/USDT balance.

    B. BUY Using Mobile / Tablets of Android / IOS

    * Metamask Wallet *

    a. Open any of the Browsers listed above. Alternatively, You may open Metamask wallet

    i. If you opened the Metamask Wallet, then in the bottom menu, click the Browsing Icon (looks like compass, 2nd last) then write our presale page URL (https://robopuppy.world/presale).

    ii. If you opened regular browser (Chrome, Brave, etc.), then go to our presale page URL (https://robopuppy.world/presale).

    b. Scroll down the page, until you see 'Connect Wallet' button and click it, click Metamask. Authenticate yourself if prompted.

    c. You will have BNB default selected. (Note: You can't select USDT as it's Disabled.)

    d. In the 'Pay BNB', give the amount of BNB you want to spend. In the next Field ('Get ROPU') You will see the amount of ROPU that you would get. Remember to have little extra BNB for Gas Fees.

    e. Click 'Buy ROPU' button. A Metamask Buy popup will appear. Check details in the popup and click 'Confirm'.

    f. Upon succesful buy, you would get an alert 'Succesfully bought ROPU'. Congrats!

    * Trust Wallet *

    a. Open Trust Wallet in your mobile. Don't open any Browser, like, Chrome, Brave, etc.

    b. Click 'Browser' icon at the bottom right corner (looks like compass). Trust Wallet Browser opens. In the Search bar, write the presale page URL (https://robopuppy.world/presale).

    c. Scroll down the page, until you see 'Connect Wallet' Button and click it, and then click Trust. Authenticate yourself if prompted.

    d. Now select your desired currency (BNB/USDT). Remember to have little BNB for Gas Fees.

    e. In the 'Pay BNB' or, 'Pay USDT' field, give the amount of the (BNB/USDT) you want to spend. In the next Field ('Get ROPU') You will see the amount of ROPU that you would get.

    f. Click 'Buy ROPU' button. A Trust Buy popup will appear. Check details in the popup and click 'Confirm'.

    g. Upon succesful buy, you would get an alert 'Succesfully bought ROPU'. Congrats!

    * Coinbase Wallet *

    a. Open any of the Browsers listed above. Alternatively, like Metamask, You can open Coinbase wallet.

    i) If you opened the Coinbase Wallet, in the Bottom Menu, click the Browser icon in the Center (looks like Web) and then write the presale page URL (https://robopuppy.world/presale) and load it.

    ii) If you opened normal Browser like, Brave, Chrome etc., Load presale page URL (https://robopuppy.world/presale).

    b. Scroll down the page, until you see 'Connect Wallet' Button and click it, then click Coinbase. Authenticate yourself if prompted.

    c. Now select your desired currency (BNB/USDT). Remember to have little BNB for Gas Fees.

    d. In the 'Pay BNB' or, 'Pay USDT' field, give the amount of the (BNB/USDT) you want to spend. In the next Field ('Get ROPU') You will see the amount of ROPU that you would get.

    e. Click 'Buy ROPU' button. A Coinbase 'Buy' popup will appear. Check details in the popup and click 'Confirm'.

    f. Upon succesful buy, you would get an alert 'Succesfully bought ROPU'. Congrats!
